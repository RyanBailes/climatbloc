Meet the ClimatBloc structural block

At the center of every ClimatBloc foundation and wall system is a single engineered block. It combines a structural internal frame, dual insulation baffles, and a passive wall core that supports airflow and sound control.

Each block is designed to be lightweight enough for crews to move by hand, yet strong enough to perform like concrete once the system is locked together and tensioned.
