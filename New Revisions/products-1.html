ClimatBloc Building Systems

A complete wall and foundation system that builds faster, insulates deeper, and lasts longer than traditional construction.

Our Building Systems

ClimatBloc replaces the old way of building with a single integrated approach. Instead of juggling framing, insulation, vapor management, and exterior structure, our interlocking blocks do it all at once.


Blocks stack quickly, lock together with a tensioning system, and create a monolithic, fully insulated shell that performs for generations. ClimatBloc products are built for builders, developers, and homeowners who want stronger buildings, predictable schedules, and lower energy bills without complicated installation.

Foundation Blocks

Fast, straight, and ready for backfill in hours

Foundation Blocks give you a structural base that goes in fast and keeps moisture under control. Crews can install a 2,000 sq ft foundation in 5 to 6 hours with a four person crew, then backfill the same day.


These foundation assemblies become the structural backbone for the entire building, delivering long lasting performance with faster schedules and lower labor costs.

Key advantages

  • Interlocking design keeps walls straight and level
  • Lightweight blocks under 30 pounds for faster handling
  • No mortar or specialized tools needed
  • Built in insulation eliminates thermal bridging
  • Vapor permeable materials reduce moisture and mold risk
  • Strong as concrete, easier to install and work with

Exterior Wall Blocks

Fully insulated wall systems that go up in days

Exterior Wall Blocks give you speed and performance in one package. They replace studs, sheathing, insulation, housewrap, vapor barriers, and exterior air sealing with a single integrated system.


The result is a continuous structural wall that stays dry, quiet, and energy efficient from day one.

Benefits for builders

  • Stack and lock walls with consistent alignment
  • Create a monolithic structure with superior strength
  • R-values from R-19 to R-84 based on climate needs
  • Passive airflow design reduces condensation
  • Excellent sound control from high mass and internal chambers
  • Fire resistant, insect resistant, and mold resistant materials

Structural Wall Panels

Pre engineered wall sections for the fastest builds

For the fastest possible installation, ClimatBloc also offers pre engineered Structural Wall Panels. These are factory built wall sections designed to your plans, delivered to the job site, and installed by crane.


In a single day, crews can lift and set the entire exterior structure and put you ahead of schedule before lunch.

Panel system advantages

  • Manufactured off site for maximum precision
  • Installed directly on slabs or subfloors
  • Minimal labor required on site
  • Rapid dry in, even on complex designs
  • Ideal for developers and large builders needing repeatable results
  • Same strength, insulation, and durability as on site block assembly

Choose Your Core Material

Every project can be matched to the material that fits the climate, budget, and performance goals. ClimatBloc systems are available in multiple substrate options so you can choose what matters most for your build.

Hemp Lime Composite

A carbon negative structural core that is breathable, fire resistant, and highly energy efficient. Hemp growth and lime curing both sequester carbon, making every block a net carbon reducer.

  • Captures 6.4 kg of CO₂ per block
  • Fire rating over one hour
  • Vapor permeable to reduce condensation and mold
  • Excellent thermal mass for stable indoor temperatures
  • Organic, mineral, and non toxic materials

AAC (Autoclaved Aerated Concrete)

A proven mineral system used worldwide. AAC offers strength, light weight, thermal mass, and excellent acoustics in one material.

  • Higher R-value per inch than standard concrete assemblies
  • Up to 80 percent air content for better insulation
  • Fire resistant and insect proof
  • Lightweight for easier handling on site
  • Superior sound reduction for quiet interiors

Carbon Black Energy Storage Core

A next generation structural material designed to store thermal energy and act as a supercapacitor. This system is in development and will be offered in select markets as testing completes.

  • Patent pending carbon black infused core
  • Stores heat at very high temperatures
  • Works with solar, wind, and other renewables
  • Expected to reduce energy costs over the long term
  • Built to integrate with high performance buildings
