About ClimatBloc™

We build wall and foundation systems that bring speed, strength, insulation, and sustainability together in one proven technology.

Our Story

ClimatBloc started with a simple reality every builder knows: projects were taking too long, energy costs kept rising, and standard wall assemblies were still fighting moisture and mold. The industry needed something better than another variation on wood framing and foam.


Our technology is built on years of engineering, patents, and real world testing. Early proof of concept homes were monitored over multiple years in demanding climates, validating structural performance, energy savings, and comfort. ClimatBloc takes that foundation and brings it into a modern, scalable system manufactured and supported here in the United States.


Today, our focus is straightforward: give builders and developers a system that installs fast, performs for generations, and aligns with the next era of sustainable construction.

Built For Modern Building Challenges

Faster Schedules

Interlocking blocks and engineered wall systems that move projects from excavation to dry in in days instead of weeks.

High R Value Performance

R values from R 19 to R 84, passive wall design, and thermal mass that keep interiors comfortable and energy use in check.

Strength And Durability

Structural systems engineered for seismic zones, high winds, fire resistance, and long term performance measured in generations.

What Sets ClimatBloc Apart

ClimatBloc is not another insulation product, it is a complete wall and foundation system. Structure, insulation, vapor management, and thermal mass work together as one assembly instead of a patchwork of trades and materials.


Our systems are designed for real job sites and real crews. Blocks are light enough to handle without special equipment, yet strong enough to serve as structural walls. Passive airflow through the core helps control moisture and reduce the risk of condensation and mold.

Sustainability is built into the materials, not added as an afterthought. Hemp lime composites, mineral based systems, and next generation energy storage cores all support a future where buildings use less energy, store more renewable power, and require fewer resources to maintain.


For builders, that translates into simpler planning, shorter timelines, and a finished structure that is easier to sell, easier to heat and cool, and easier to live in for decades.
