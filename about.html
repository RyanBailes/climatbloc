Our Story

At Calimatbloc, we saw the same problem every builder knows: tight budgets, long schedules, high energy bills. So we rolled up our sleeves and engineered a better way.

Our interlocking block system was born right here in the USA - built by designers and machinists who’ve been on job-sites. We set out to make a building process that doesn’t compromise speed for strength, or insulation for sustainability.
