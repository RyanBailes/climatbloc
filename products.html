Climate Custom R-Values & Passive Wall Systems

Insulation & Customized R-Values

ClimatBlocs are specifically designed to meet the needs and requirements of every climate on earth. Each block is designed and manufactured with two insulation baffles, one on the interior and one on the exterior of the frame. Each baffle can be injected with 2.5" of our foam insulation, providing an R-Value of R-19. If you are building in a temperate climate, R-19 may be perfect, however if your location demands more insulation, we can fill both baffles for R-40 or if you live in the frigid arctic we will fill the entire block for an industry leading R-84.

Passive Wall Design

ClimatBlocs have a patent pending passive wall design. This means that our wall systems are designed to maintain constant airflow through the core of the wall. The perpetual airflow not only increases the R-Value and performance of the wall, it also eliminates condensation and the mold that often forms in standard wood framed walls. The open chamber of the wall also acts as a sound barrier, greatly reducing outside noise.
